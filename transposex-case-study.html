Transpose X Case Study

Cross-Platform Mobile App with Optical Music Recognition (OMR)

Transpose X is a fully working application designed to help musicians digitize and manipulate sheet music. The project combines a Flutter-based mobile frontend with a Node.js backend powered by the Audiveris OMR engine to transform images of sheet music into editable MusicXML files.

Problem

Musicians often need to digitize sheet music for practice, editing, or transposition. Manual transcription is slow and error-prone, with few modern mobile solutions available.

Solution

Transpose X enables users to snap a photo of sheet music, automatically convert it to MusicXML, and instantly transpose it into any key ‚Äî all within a clean mobile app interface. The prototype demonstrates how professional-grade OMR and custom-built algorithms can run seamlessly on a consumer-facing app.
